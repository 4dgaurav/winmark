Aakrit Vaish

Aakrit Vaish is a Director at Winmark, looking at overall digital strategy and operations. After completing his Bachelor's in Industrial Engineering from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in 2008, Aakrit has been involved with various ventures in the IT sector. In 2010, he started working at Flurry Inc, a Silicon Valley based mobile advertising company, one of the world's largest in the space. In 2011, he moved to India to start Flurry's operations in the region, and grew the team to 10 by 2013. Along the way, he started investing in Indian IT start ups, and has made 6 investments in various sectors including advertising, consumer internet, and enterprise software. In 2013, he started a new venture called Haptik, a mobile app company doing customer support and now spends most of his time focused on that. He has been instrumental in the digital activities of Winmark's home business including e-commerce, social media and advertising.

Ajay Vaish

An Entrepreneur with a zest for innovative ideas, Mr. Ajay Vaish, the Chairman & Managing Director of the Company, brings with him a rich & varied portfolio of experience of more than three decades, creativity & marketing being his key strength which has bought all the value to the organization. He has also built an attitude of perfectionism in every team member which helps us in meeting the customer demands of excellence in quality.
